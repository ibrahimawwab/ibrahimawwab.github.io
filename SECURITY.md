## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a **security vulnerability** in this repository, please follow the instructions below. These guidelines are designed to **ensure responsible disclosure** while keeping all parties secure.

### Step 1: Direct Email Reporting

Send a detailed report to the official professional email:  

- **Email:** [ibrahimawwab@proton.me](mailto:ibrahimawwab@proton.me)  
- **Subject Line:** `Security Vulnerability Report – ibrahimawwab.github.io`  

### Step 2: Required Information

Include the following details to help evaluate the vulnerability:  

1. A clear and concise **description of the issue**  
2. **Steps to reproduce** the vulnerability  
3. **Impact assessment** (who or what may be affected)  
4. **Screenshots, logs, or any supporting evidence** if applicable  
5. Any **suggested solutions or workarounds**  

### Step 3: Confidentiality

- Do **not post security issues in public GitHub issues, discussions, or social media**, as this may expose users to risk.  
- All reports will be **handled confidentially** by Dr. Muhammad Ibrahim Awwab.

## Response and Resolution Policy

Dr. Muhammad Ibrahim Awwab is committed to **professional, timely, and responsible handling of all reported security issues**:

1. **Acknowledgment:** You will receive confirmation of receipt within **5 business days**.  
2. **Assessment:** The vulnerability will be evaluated and prioritized based on **risk severity and potential impact**.  
3. **Mitigation:** Appropriate actions will be taken, including **patching, workflow updates, or content revisions**.  
4. **Follow-up:** You will receive regular updates until the vulnerability is resolved.  
5. **Transparency:** Once resolved, high-impact issues may be publicly documented with **disclosure of the resolution methodology**.
